SKILLS FOR life!

Capibility aims to inspire students to embrace the joy of learning while equipping them to become visionary 21st-century leaders!

Why Capybara?

Symbol of Relaxation and Calmness: Capybaras are often associated with relaxation and calmness. They are known for their gentle and easygoing nature. We want convey the message that learning and self-improvement can be a stress-free and enjoyable experience, just like being around capybaras.

Become a Mentor

Mentor other students with life's essential skills like time management , networking , managing your workload , and building effective study habits , instilling the knowledge and confidence necessary for a balanced and successful life.
