Introduction to the Portrait of a Graduate and the Transversal Skills for Success

What are transversal competencies?

Transversal competencies are non-technical, interpersonal skills that enable individuals to interact effectively with others and work efficiently to solve real-world problems.

They include communication, teamwork, critical thinking, adaptability, and time management.

Expectations for Cégep Students

The project has many different elements. This particular element, the Portrait of a Graduate, aims to more clearly define what it means to be “competent” (i.e. awesome, confident, capable, etc.) for our students in these skills.

Our belief is that if our Vanier community could find some common ground on how to define these skills and what it means to be “good” at them. Students, teachers and community members could be even more motivated to develop these skills inside and outside of the classroom. 

This project was funded by Entente-Canada Quebec to propose different ways to help students develop transversal skills both in and outside of the classroom.
